# User Journey Maps & Flow Diagrams

## 1. Customer Journey Maps

### Primary Customer Journey: First-Time Booking

**Persona:** Jennifer, 28, Marketing Professional
**Goal:** Find a trusted hair stylist for a special event
**Context:** Looking for a professional haircut for an upcoming work presentation

#### Journey Stages:

**1. AWARENESS (Pre-Platform)**
- **Trigger:** Realizes need for professional haircut
- **Thoughts:** "I need a good stylist, but don't know who to trust"
- **Emotions:** Uncertain, slightly anxious
- **Actions:** 
  - Asks friends for recommendations
  - Searches Google for "hair salons near me"
  - Discovers booking platform through search/referral

**2. DISCOVERY (Landing → Search)**
- **Touchpoints:** Landing page, search interface
- **Thoughts:** "This looks professional, let me see what's available"
- **Emotions:** Cautiously optimistic
- **Actions:**
  - Reads value propositions on landing page
  - Uses search bar to find "hair stylists near me"
  - Browses categories and featured specialists
- **Pain Points:** 
  - Too many options can be overwhelming
  - Needs trust indicators
- **Opportunities:**
  - Clear filtering options
  - Prominent trust badges
  - Social proof integration

**3. EVALUATION (Search Results → Specialist Profiles)**
- **Touchpoints:** Search results, specialist profiles, reviews
- **Thoughts:** "Which stylist has the style I want and good reviews?"
- **Emotions:** Analytical, comparing options
- **Actions:**
  - Filters by location, price, and rating
  - Views multiple specialist profiles
  - Reads reviews and examines portfolios
  - Compares availability and pricing
- **Pain Points:**
  - Difficulty comparing specialists
  - Lack of portfolio examples
  - Unclear pricing structure
- **Opportunities:**
  - Comparison tool
  - Rich media portfolios
  - Transparent pricing
  - Video introductions

**4. DECISION (Profile → Booking Flow)**
- **Touchpoints:** Booking widget, service selection, calendar
- **Thoughts:** "Sarah seems perfect, let me book with her"
- **Emotions:** Confident, ready to commit
- **Actions:**
  - Selects preferred specialist (Sarah)
  - Chooses service and add-ons
  - Picks available date/time
  - Enters personal details
- **Pain Points:**
  - Complex booking process
  - Hidden fees
  - Limited availability
- **Opportunities:**
  - Streamlined booking flow
  - Real-time availability
  - Clear pricing breakdown
  - Progress indicators

**5. COMMITMENT (Payment → Confirmation)**
- **Touchpoints:** Payment form, confirmation page, email
- **Thoughts:** "This deposit system feels safe"
- **Emotions:** Excited, slightly nervous about new stylist
- **Actions:**
  - Pays deposit securely
  - Receives booking confirmation
  - Adds appointment to calendar
  - Receives confirmation email
- **Pain Points:**
  - Payment security concerns
  - Unclear next steps
  - No direct communication with specialist
- **Opportunities:**
  - Multiple payment options
  - Immediate specialist contact
  - Clear next steps
  - Calendar integration

**6. ANTICIPATION (Confirmation → Appointment)**
- **Touchpoints:** Email reminders, SMS, specialist messages
- **Thoughts:** "I'm looking forward to this, hope it goes well"
- **Emotions:** Anticipatory, slightly anxious
- **Actions:**
  - Receives booking confirmation from specialist
  - Gets reminder notifications
  - May message specialist with questions
  - Prepares for appointment
- **Pain Points:**
  - Lack of communication
  - Uncertainty about location/parking
  - No preparation guidance
- **Opportunities:**
  - Automated reminders
  - Specialist preparation tips
  - Location/parking info
  - Pre-appointment chat

**7. SERVICE (During Appointment)**
- **Touchpoints:** In-person service, potential app interactions
- **Thoughts:** "The service is going great, this was worth it"
- **Emotions:** Relaxed, satisfied, confident
- **Actions:**
  - Receives professional service
  - May receive service updates via app
  - Pays remaining balance
  - Takes photos of results
- **Pain Points:**
  - Payment friction
  - No way to document experience
- **Opportunities:**
  - Seamless payment integration
  - Photo sharing features
  - Real-time feedback option

**8. POST-SERVICE (After Appointment)**
- **Touchpoints:** Payment completion, review request, follow-up
- **Thoughts:** "That was amazing, I'll definitely book again"
- **Emotions:** Delighted, confident, loyal
- **Actions:**
  - Completes payment
  - Leaves review and rating
  - Shares experience on social media
  - Books follow-up appointment
- **Pain Points:**
  - Complicated review process
  - No easy rebooking option
- **Opportunities:**
  - One-click review submission
  - Instant rebooking option
  - Social sharing integration
  - Loyalty program enrollment

## 2. Specialist Journey Maps

### Primary Specialist Journey: Platform Onboarding to First Booking

**Persona:** Sarah, 32, Independent Hair Stylist
**Goal:** Grow client base and streamline booking management
**Context:** Established stylist looking to expand beyond walk-ins and referrals

#### Journey Stages:

**1. AWARENESS**
- **Trigger:** Business growth challenges, manual booking hassles
- **Thoughts:** "I need a better way to manage bookings and find new clients"
- **Emotions:** Frustrated with current system, hopeful for solution
- **Actions:**
  - Researches booking platforms
  - Compares features and pricing
  - Reads reviews from other specialists

**2. EVALUATION**
- **Touchpoints:** Marketing site, competitor comparisons, demos
- **Thoughts:** "Will this platform really help my business?"
- **Emotions:** Cautious, analytical
- **Actions:**
  - Requests demo or free trial
  - Reviews commission structure
  - Checks client quality and volume
  - Reads terms and conditions

**3. ONBOARDING**
- **Touchpoints:** Registration, profile setup, verification
- **Thoughts:** "This setup process should be straightforward"
- **Emotions:** Motivated, slightly overwhelmed
- **Actions:**
  - Creates account and completes profile
  - Uploads portfolio photos
  - Sets up services and pricing
  - Completes verification process
  - Configures availability calendar

**4. FIRST LISTING**
- **Touchpoints:** Profile review, search visibility, optimization tips
- **Thoughts:** "I hope clients will find and choose me"
- **Emotions:** Nervous excitement, uncertain
- **Actions:**
  - Reviews profile completeness
  - Adjusts pricing and availability
  - Optimizes profile based on platform suggestions
  - Waits for first inquiries

**5. FIRST BOOKING**
- **Touchpoints:** Booking notification, client communication, calendar
- **Thoughts:** "Great! My first booking through the platform"
- **Emotions:** Excited, validated
- **Actions:**
  - Receives booking request notification
  - Reviews client details and requests
  - Confirms booking
  - Prepares for appointment

**6. SERVICE DELIVERY**
- **Touchpoints:** Appointment management, payment processing
- **Thoughts:** "The platform makes payment so easy"
- **Emotions:** Confident, professional
- **Actions:**
  - Provides service to client
  - Processes payment through platform
  - Updates appointment status
  - Handles any service issues

**7. POST-SERVICE**
- **Touchpoints:** Review system, earnings tracking, rebooking
- **Thoughts:** "Positive review! This client might become regular"
- **Emotions:** Satisfied, optimistic about growth
- **Actions:**
  - Receives client review
  - Responds to feedback
  - Tracks earnings and metrics
  - Follows up for future bookings

**8. GROWTH & OPTIMIZATION**
- **Touchpoints:** Analytics dashboard, performance insights, marketing tools
- **Thoughts:** "How can I improve my booking rate and earnings?"
- **Emotions:** Strategic, growth-focused
- **Actions:**
  - Reviews performance analytics
  - Optimizes pricing and availability
  - Improves profile and portfolio
  - Uses platform marketing features
