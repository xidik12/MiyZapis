# Platform Comparison: MiyZapis vs Panhaha

## Executive Summary

Both platforms are **booking/appointment management systems** with nearly identical feature sets and architecture. The primary difference is the **color scheme and branding**.

---

## Color Schemes

### MiyZapis
- **Primary**: Sky Blue (#3b97f2) - Light, friendly, approachable
- **Secondary**: Bright Yellow (#ffc41f) - Energetic, optimistic, cheerful
- **Overall Feel**: Bright, cheerful, approachable - perfect for general services

### Panhaha ✅
- **Primary**: Dark Navy Blue (#1E40AF) - Professional, trustworthy, deep
- **Secondary**: Bright Crimson Red (#DC2626) - Bold, energetic, action-oriented
- **Accent**: Rich Gold (#EAB308) - Premium feel (borders/outlines only)
- **Overall Feel**: Professional, bold, premium - excellent for professional services

**Status**: ✅ Panhaha correctly uses Blue and Red color scheme as requested

## Design System Comparison

### Typography
**MiyZapis**:
- Font: Inter + JetBrains Mono
- Standard hierarchy

**Panhaha**:
- Font: Inter + Space Grotesk (display) + JetBrains Mono
- Standard hierarchy
- **Advantage**: Additional display font for hero sections

### Border Radius
**MiyZapis**:
- sm: 0.25rem
- md: 0.375rem
- lg: 0.5rem
- xl: 0.75rem
- 2xl: 1rem

**Panhaha**:
- sm: 6px
- md: 10px
- lg: 14px
- xl: 18px
- 2xl: 24px
- **Advantage**: Slightly more rounded for modern feel

### Shadows
Both platforms have comprehensive shadow systems including:
- Standard shadows (sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl)
- Color-specific shadows (primary, secondary, error, success)
- Glass effects
- Glow effects

**Panhaha Additions**:
- `shadow-soft`: Subtle elevation
- `shadow-elevated`: Enhanced depth
- `glow-accent`: Gold outline glow
